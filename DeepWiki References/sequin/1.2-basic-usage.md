Common Issues and Tips
----------------------

1.  **Missing ANSI sequences**: Many programs don't output ANSI sequences when output isn't a terminal. Force colored output with program-specific options like `--color=always` or environment variables like `CLICOLOR_FORCE=1`.
    
2.  **Terminal compatibility**: Some sequences may behave differently depending on your terminal. Sequin explains the sequences according to standard specifications.
    
3.  **Complex programs**: For complex TUIs, output may be very verbose. Use raw mode (`-r`) for a more concise view.
